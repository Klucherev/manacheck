What is manacheck

Manacheck is a service for Magic:the Gathering, that helps you to create a perfect manabase for your deck. It emulates first rounds of the game 1000 times trying to obtain given combination of colors. It tries to figure best order of lands that will achieve goal as fast as possible. You can share result simply providing the link.

Simulation rules
  • Mulligan, if hand has only one land or more than five lands and hand size is more than 5
  • Deck size is 60 or 40, may be changed in left tab
  • You may select "type" of spells, so lands giving some types of mana for spells of the chosen type will provide it correctly
  • If a land needs some kind of resource (life, artifact in play, etc) assume, that condition is satisfied
Fill your manabase
  • Select at least 15 lands on lands tab
  • Similar lands count restricted to a maximum of 4, with exception for basic lands

Select mana combination
  • Select any mana combination (at least one mana symbol)
  • You'll get an error if your manabase can't provide given mana combination
