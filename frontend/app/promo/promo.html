What is manacheck

Manacheck is a service for Magic:the Gathering, that helps you to create a perfect manabase for your deck. It emulates first rounds of the game 1000 times trying to obdain given combination of colors. It tries to figure best order of lands that will achieve goal as fast as possible. It doesn't cheat, all information it has based on given hand and given manabase, each turn he gets one card, like in a real game.

Simulation rules
  • Mulligan, if hand has more than 5 cards and lands count is more than 5 or less than 2
  • Deck size is 60 or 40, may be changed in left tab
  • You may select "type", so lands giving some types of mana for spells of the choosen type will provide it correctly
  • If a land needs some kind of recource(life, artifact in play, etc) assume, that condition is satisfied
Fill your manabase
  • Select at least 15 lands on lands tab
  • Similar lands count restricted to a maximum of 4, with exeption for basic lands
